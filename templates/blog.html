Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever...

The Problem

The project began with recognizing a common issue: people often experience symptoms and want quick answers...

The Solution

We developed a web app that allows users to enter a list of symptoms they are experiencing...

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through a user-friendly interface.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier predicts the disease.
  3. Prediction: The app instantly provides possible diagnoses.
  4. Additional Information: Details about each predicted disease are also shown.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy navigation and accessibility.
  • Accurate Predictions: Based on a robust ML model.
  • Comprehensive Information: Descriptions, precautions, and more.
  • Educational Content: Includes informative health blog posts.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App brings the power of machine learning and healthcare information...
